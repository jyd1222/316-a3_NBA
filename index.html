From Paint to Perimeter

How the NBA became a three-point league

This explorable follows how league offense shifted toward the arc, then lets you compare which teams embraced the change early and which held on to older shot profiles.

Start with the league trend, scrub the season slider, then hover or click a team in the scatterplot to trace its path against the league average.

Start here

Watch the line chart first. Then use the selected season to see how widely teams were spread between perimeter-heavy and more traditional offenses.

About this visualization. Rather than acting like a general NBA dashboard, this project stays focused on one question: how quickly did teams move from paint-oriented offense to a three-point-heavy style, and how uneven was that transition across the league?
