From Paint to Perimeter

How the NBA became a three-point league

This explorable tracks how NBA offense moved toward the arc, then lets you compare which teams adopted perimeter-heavy shot profiles early and which resisted the shift.

Start with the league trend, scrub the season slider, then hover or click a team to carry that comparison through the other views.

Start here

Watch the league curve first. The scatterplot then shows how wide the spread was in that season, and the bottom chart turns one franchise into a longer historical comparison.

About this visualization. Rather than acting like a general NBA dashboard, this project stays focused on one question: how quickly did teams move from paint-oriented offense to a three-point-heavy style, and how uneven was that transition across the league?
